Bitcoin

I first started accepting Bitcoin for freelance work in the summer of 2013. Since then I've been following the developments closely. I recently decided to focus all of my time and energy on the space. Here's a collection of the small projects I've been working on.

MaiCoin's Email (under NDA) · Feb 24th 2014

I worked with the team at MaiCoin to create responsive transactional emails that will be sent to their users. Unfortunately I can't show the work yet but I really enjoyed using the Ink framework and Roadie gem to get the job done.

KikCoin Hackathon Project · Feb 23rd 2014

I attended the SF Bitcoindev Hackathon where I partnered up with Kevin Greene to work on his idea for web-based mobile app for Coinbase that could work within the Kik Messenger.

Blockchain.info homepage concept · Feb 20th 2014

BitGo site idea · Feb 11th 2014
